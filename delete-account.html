How to delete your account

Send a deletion request by email

Email us from the address associated with your Olfly account. This lets us verify your identity before permanently deleting your data.

  1. 1 Use the button below to open a pre-filled email, or compose one manually to support@olfly.app.
  2. 2 Send the email from the address you used to sign up for Olfly (Google or email/password).
  3. 3 We'll confirm your request and permanently delete your account and data within 30 days.

Your data

What happens to your data

When your account is deleted, here is exactly what is removed and what is temporarily retained.

🗑 Permanently deleted
  • Your account profile and email
  • Login credentials and tokens
  • All training session history
  • Your personal smell diary
  • Perception logs and notes
  • Progress, streaks, and settings
  • Notification preferences
🔒 Temporarily kept
  • Anonymized, non-identifiable usage data
  • Security and fraud prevention logs
  • Transactional records required by law
Retention period: Anonymized usage data and security logs are retained for up to 90 days after deletion for fraud prevention and legal compliance. This data cannot be linked back to you. After 90 days, everything is permanently purged from our systems.
All deletion requests are processed within 30 days
Keep your account

Remove specific data without closing your account

You don't have to delete your entire account to remove data. From within Olfly you can:

  1. ✓ Delete individual symptom log entries directly from the journal screen.
  2. ✓ Remove custom scent collections you've created from the library.
  3. ✓ Email support@olfly.app to request removal of specific data only — without closing your account.
